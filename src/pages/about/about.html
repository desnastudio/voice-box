About

The VoiceBox is the online voice recorder that may be used not only for recording corporate meetings and personal suggestions, but also for Health Care industry and Low Cases.

During development process our engineers achieved the following parameters:

  • Sampling rate 44100 Hz
  • Bitrate 128 kbps

Such quality fits the requirements that may appear during recording for any low cases or protocol important conversations.

The VoiceBox traps each voice wave and change.
